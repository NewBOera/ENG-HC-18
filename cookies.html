2. The Scoop on Digital Cookies

Cookies are tiny bits of text that get stored on your device when you visit our site. They’re like little helpers that make your browsing experience better. Here’s what you need to know:

  • Session Cookies: Think of these as short-term visitors. They stick around only while you’re on our site and vanish when you close your browser. They’re crucial for things like keeping you logged in and remembering your preferences during your visit.
  • Persistent Cookies: These are like old friends who hang around for a while. They stay on your device even after you’ve left our site, remembering your settings and preferences for your next visit.
